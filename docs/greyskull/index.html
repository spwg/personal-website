People looking for their first weightlifting routine usually have a problem keeping track of workouts. They typically use spreadsheets on small phones and/or take a notepad/pencil to the gym.

Greyskull LP is the recommended workout routine of r/Fitness for beginners. So, it has a lot first time weightlifters.

This app simplifies the process of going to the gym, working out, and coming home. It allows you to track workouts in the routine. You can log workouts and set timers between sets so you know when to start your next set.

It is intentionally simple because I wanted to complete it fast. It took about nine days to write, test, and submit to the app store.
