Greyskull LP mobile app

Track your workout to achieve goals faster

Privacy Policy

People looking for their first weightlifting routine usually have a problem keeping track of workouts. They typically use spreadsheets on small phones and/or take a notepad/pencil to the gym.

Greyskull LP is the recommended workout routine of r/Fitness for beginners [1]. So, it has a lot first time weightlifters.

This app simplifies the process of going to the gym, working out, and coming home. It allows you to track workouts in the routine. You can log workouts and set timers between sets so you know when to start your next set.

It is intentionally simple because I wanted to ship it fast. It took about nine days to write, test, and submit to the app store.

From the beginning, I strived to make the code testable, keep the dependency list small, and focus on core features. As a result, I achieved 88.76% line coverage and actually shipped the app in a reasonable amount of time despite this being my only the 2nd app I've made. To potentially release both Android and iOS I chose to use React Native.
